About

What is GCore?

GCore is a generator tycoon experience built from the ground up. Place generators on your plot, collect resources, sell them for profit, and upgrade your way to the top of the leaderboards.

With a full prestige system, custom pets, private mines, farming, fishing, PvP arenas, and hundreds of generators to unlock — there's always something to grind for.

Features
Everything You Need to Dominate

A full tycoon experience packed with custom content.

⚙

60 Generator Tiers

Upgrade through 30 money generators and 30 token forges. Each tier drops more resources, and upgrading is the core path to building your empire.

★

Gen Pilers

Stack multiple generators into a single block with a massive multiplier boost. 8 tiers from Common (1.15x) all the way up to Admin (4.0x), holding up to 150 gens in one block.

🐾

Tycoon Pets

Hatch eggs and discover 28 unique pets from Common to Mythic rarity. Each pet provides passive resource drips and tycoon abilities to boost your grind.

⛏

Prestige & Quests

Prestige to earn permanent multipliers, bonus gen slots, and prestige points. Complete daily quests and mastery challenges for extra rewards.

🌱

Farming, Fishing & Mines

Harvest 68 different crops in /farm with your upgradeable Harvester tool. Fish at the pond with upgradeable rods and mutation catalyst. Mine in your own tiered private mine with custom pickaxe enchantments.

⭐

Reputation

Spend eco to earn Rep in a weekly competition. Top 10 players win Credits every Saturday, and 1st place takes home real cash prizes alongside their Credits.
