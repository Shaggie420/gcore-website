🌏 Server Overview

Essential

GCore is a generator tycoon server on Minehut. Place generators on your plot, sell resources, prestige, and compete to become the richest player on the server.

How to Join

Java Edition 1.21+ — connect to gcore.minehut.gg

First Steps

1. Run /help

The 10-step interactive guide walks you through every feature and gives you free crate keys as rewards for each step completed.

2. Visit your Plot

Type /p h to go to your plot. This is where you place generators.

3. Buy Generators

Open /shop to buy your first generators. Place them on your plot and they auto-drop resources that sell on pickup.

Complete all 10 steps of /help to earn a Genesis Key — the rarest key type in the game.

⚙ Generators

Essential

Generators are the core of GCore. Place them on your plot and they periodically drop items that auto-sell when you walk over them. There are 30 money generators and 30 token forges (60 total tiers).

How They Work

Placing & Collecting

Buy generators from /shop, place them on your plot (/p h). Generator drops auto-sell on pickup. You can also use sellwands to sell items stored in chests.

Money Generators vs Token Forges

Money generators produce eco (your main currency). Token forges produce tokens (secondary currency used in the token shop). Both have 30 tiers each. View all tiers and their stats in /index.

Upgrading

Higher tier generators cost more but produce significantly more per drop. Money gen upgrades cost eco, token forge upgrades cost tokens. Upgrade through the /index menu.

Gen Slots

You have a limited number of gen slots. Earn more slots through prestige, rebirth, quests, and the credit shop. Pilers let you stack multiple generators into one slot.

Gen drops auto-sell on pickup! Just walk over them. Use sellwands to bulk-sell items in chests.

📦 Gen Pilers

Important

Gen Pilers let you stack multiple generators into a single block. The stacked generators all produce together with a multiplier bonus based on the piler tier. This is one of the most powerful progression tools in the game.

How Pilers Work

A piler has a capacity (how many gens it holds) and a multiplier (bonus applied to all gens inside). Higher tier pilers hold more gens and give bigger multiplier boosts. Piled generators still count toward your gen slot limit — this is intentional design.

⭐ Prestige

Essential

Prestige is how you permanently progress. Once you reach the required level, you can prestige to gain permanent bonuses. Command: /prestige

Requirements

Each prestige costs a certain number of levels. The formula is: 10 + (current prestige × 5) levels. So Prestige 0→1 costs Level 10, Prestige 1→2 costs Level 15, Prestige 5→6 costs Level 35, and so on.

Rewards Per Prestige

Prestige early and often. The gen slots alone make each prestige worth it, and the multiplier compounds over time.

💎 Rebirth

Important

Rebirth is the ultimate ascension beyond prestige. It gives massive rewards including gen slots, relics, and a pity-based jackpot system. Commands: /rebirth, /skills

Jackpot Pity System

Every rebirth rolls a random reward. If you go 10 rebirths without hitting the jackpot, the pity system guarantees it on your next rebirth. Rewards range from +5 gen slots (common) to god-tier relics and genesis keys.

🌱 Farming

Important

Farming is one of the fastest ways to gain XP and level up. Travel to the farm with /farm and harvest crops using your Harvester tool.

How it Works

There are 68 different crops in the game. New crops unlock as you prestige. You harvest them by left-clicking with your Harvester tool in the farming area. Each crop gives XP and eco.

The Harvester

Your Harvester is a special tool that levels up as you use it. Higher levels increase your harvest speed and rewards. You can upgrade it faster through the farm shop.

Farmer Dan

Talk to Farmer Dan at the farm for information, upgrades, and special purchases. He is your main NPC for all farming-related content.

Farming is the fastest way to gain levels for your next prestige. Always farm between prestiges to level up quickly.

⛏ Private Mines

Important

Your private mine is a personal mining area where you break blocks for eco, tokens, and rare finds. Access it with /pmine. Talk to Forger Dan for pickaxe upgrades.

Mine Tiers

Your mine can be upgraded through tiers, unlocking better ores and bigger mining areas. Each tier resets the mine with new block compositions.

Pickaxe Enchantments

Your pickaxe has 3 tabs of enchantments. Shift-click with your pickaxe to open the enchant menu. Enchantments cost Essence (mined from blocks) to upgrade.

⚔ PvP / FFA

Moderate

Enter the Battle world with /ffa for free-for-all PvP combat. Gear up in the /armory with custom kits.

Combat Tagging

When you hit or get hit by another player, you are combat tagged for 15 seconds. During this time you cannot use teleport commands (/spawn, /farm, /tpa, etc). An action bar shows your combat timer.

Other PvP Modes

Besides FFA, there is also /ctf (Capture the Flag) and /duels (1v1 challenges).

🤝 Trading

Moderate

Trade safely with other players using /trade <player>. The system supports items plus 6 currencies in a single trade.

What You Can Trade

Items (including pets), Money, Tokens, Crystals, Essence, Points, and Reputation. Both players get a 12-slot item grid plus currency controls.

Anti-Scam Protection

The trading system has built-in pet verification that detects fake or duplicated pets automatically, replacing them with a warning barrier. Both players must confirm before the trade executes, and balance checks prevent trading currencies you don't have.

🎈 Events

Moderate

GCore runs seasonal events with unique currencies, shops, and rewards. Access with /event.

Bee Event

Visit the Bee Keeper NPC to earn pollen-based currencies (Amber, Crimson, Void). Buy Common Gen Pilers (daily limit of 5), and forge the Hive Mind Core for +0.5 permanent multiplier, +5 gen slots, and a Mythic Piler.

Spring Event

The Para Tree has a level progression system. Level up by completing spring tasks. Rewards include Common Pilers (levels 1-4, 6-9), +1 max gen slot (every 5 levels), and +0.1x multiplier (every 10 levels). Catch fairies for extra loot.

Minigames

Squid Games, Spleef, and Paintball rotate as event minigames with their own rewards.

🏷 Tags

Optional

Tags are cosmetic badges that show next to your name in chat and TAB. Unlock them through paid ranks, generator milestones, and achievements. Equip and switch tags from the tag GUI.

Tags are purely cosmetic and do not affect gameplay. They are a way to show off your progress and purchases.
